Faction Spotlight

Choose your allegiance in the battle for the Star.

The Ironbound Union

"Steel binds what the Aether breaks."

Masters of industry and defensive fortification. The Ironbound do not just survive the fracture; they bolt it together. They excel at generating raw Iron and holding territory against aggressive incursions.

Defense High
Economy Medium
Mobility Low

The Flesh-Bound Horde

"Consume. Adapt. Overrun."

A biological nightmare born from Aether toxicity. The Horde ignores supply lines, paying for units with their own health. They swarm the map early, overwhelming opponents before defenses can be raised.

Defense Low
Economy Low
Offense Extreme

The Chrono-Walkers

"Time is a path, not a wall."

Nomads displaced from a future that never happened. They manipulate turn order and can "blink" units across the map. Highly mobile but fragile, they rely on hit-and-run tactics to steal victory.

Defense Low
Economy Medium
Mobility Extreme

The Verdant Silence

"The roots run deeper than steel."

Guardians of the floating forests who use Aether to accelerate growth. They can spawn terrain tiles and trap opponents in overgrowth. A control faction that reshapes the map to their advantage.

Defense Medium
Control High
Mobility Medium

The Gilded Synod

"Everything has a price."

The merchant princes who fund the war. They generate immense wealth and can buy units directly from opponents or bribe mercenaries. They win by out-spending and out-producing everyone else.

Defense Low
Economy Extreme
Mobility Low

The Astral Covenant

"We are starlight made manifest."

Cultists who worship the Shattered Star. They use pure Aether to cast devastating board-wide spells, glassing entire regions. High risk, high reward glass cannons.

Defense Very Low
Magic Extreme
Economy High

The Obsidian Core

"Unbreakable. Unstoppable."

Living golems hewn from the bedrock of the floating isles. They are incredibly slow but nearly impossible to kill. They advance like a glacier, crushing everything in their path.

Defense Extreme
Economy Low
Mobility Very Low
